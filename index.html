About me
WHO IS FAITH?

Meet Rochelyn Fatima C. Manada, or Faith as she's fondly called, a 20-year-old Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Gordon College in Olongapo City. Born on March 16, 2004, Faith is a multifaceted individual with a deep-seated passion for technology, the arts, and community engagement. Alongside her academic pursuits, she finds solace and joy in expressing herself through singing, capturing moments through photography, and unleashing her creativity in designing.

 

Whether she's immersed in the intricacies of coding languages, composing melodies, framing the perfect shot, or crafting innovative designs, Faith approaches each endeavor with equal fervor and dedication. Her commitment to excellence extends beyond her personal interests, as she actively contributes to community initiatives, leveraging her skills to make a positive impact. With her diverse talents, unwavering determination, and compassionate spirit, Faith embodies the essence of a modern-day Renaissance woman, poised to make meaningful contributions to both the tech industry and society at large.
